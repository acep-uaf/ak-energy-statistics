Quality and Outliers

In order to provide the most accurate version of this essential data, we performed two levels of checks. First, we set up and enforced basic boundary rules to ensure the data was within reasonable bounds. Second, we set up and ran statistical analysis in order to drop extreme anamolies within the data. For transparency, logs of the the removed records are shown below.
